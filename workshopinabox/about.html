Release Early, Release Often

Over the past several years the Greenpeace Story team has created folders and folders of content. One of their creations is a series of exercises and slideshows lovingly titled "Workshop in a Box". This site aims to make those resources easier for everyone to use.

This site isn't "finished", it's started. With the years worth of content created by the Greenpeace Story Team, Laura was able to design, develop and put together this site, its graphics and snippets. This first iteration is just that, a beginning. If you want to provide some feedback, please come to a community call or get in touch with Laura.

Want to chat?

We hold biweekly community calls, which you are welcome to join.
