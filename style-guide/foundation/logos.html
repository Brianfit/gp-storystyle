The Greenpeace Logo

The Master Artwork for the Greenpeace logo is at: greenpeace.org/identity. For more logo guides click here

  • Variations Please do reproduce the logo from master artwork. Please don't redraw or alter the logo.
  • Minimum size Please make sure the logo is not smaller than 30mm (85px). This should only be used where space is limited.
  • Spacing Please do allow the logo some space to breath. A good rule of thumb is to make sure nothing else intercepts the space around the logo equal to half the height of the logo. E.g. Spacing should be at least 15mm all the way around.
  • Shape Well-known international logos rely on their shape rather than their lettering for recognition. They become symbols rather than written words. Altering the shape can confuse people, especially non-native English speakers.
